## 💡 Tips for Best Results

1. **Warm up first**: Do a practice round
2. **Focus on accuracy**: Speed comes with practice
3. **Use proper posture**: Sit up straight
4. **Home row position**: ASDF and JKL;
5. **Don't look down**: Build muscle memory
6. **Practice daily**: 15-30 minutes recommended
7. **Review slowest words**: Target weak areas
8. **Track consistency**: Aim for steady rhythm

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### Graph not showing?
- Check "Live WPM Graph" in settings
- Refresh the page
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled

### Achievements not unlocking?
- Complete full 60-second tests
- Check browser localStorage is enabled
- Clear cache and try again

### Custom text not working?
- Ensure text is saved in settings
- Select "Custom" mode
- Text must be at least 10 words
