How to Type Faster

1

Proper Posture

Sit up straight with feet flat on the floor. Keep your wrists elevated and straight.

2

Home Row Position

Place your fingers on ASDF and JKL; keys. This is your starting position.

3

Don't Look Down

Keep your eyes on the screen, not the keyboard. Build muscle memory.

4

Practice Daily

Consistency is key. Practice 15-30 minutes daily for best results.
